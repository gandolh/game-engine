## The cozy contract

> *A cozy placement puzzle you read by watching the town live.*

The rule of record ‚Äî **nothing you built is taken from you**. Threats don't
destroy; they dent local happiness, which taxes productivity down to a ~60‚Äì70%
floor (never zero), so recovery is always guaranteed and there's no death spiral.

The old destructive behaviour isn't gone ‚Äî it's frozen byte-identical behind
`cozyThreats: false` and re-pointed to autonomous sharp-mode systems, powering a
solo-only **Challenge mode**.

## The two-way service loop

The economy hangs on one curve, `bufferServiceFactor`, sampled on an EWMA of
service:

```text
0.6  ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚ñ∂  1.0  ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚îÄ‚ñ∂  1.25
stockpile-pressure   neutral      service-bonus
throttle (floor)                  (a building clearing
                                   the 0.75 service band)
```

Producers are single-slot; happiness alone tops out at 0.9, and service carries
the last 0.1 of the immigration roll ‚Äî so a well-served town measurably
out-produces a starved one (~2.08√ó) without anything ever hitting zero.

<Aside type="tip" title="A mode is a call-site preset, never sim state">
  The sim only takes flat, independent options (`cozyThreats`, `enableArmy`,
  `seedTown`, `chargeBuildCost`, ‚Ä¶). "Cozy" and "Challenge" are just *bundles* of
  those ‚Äî and every mode-affecting option must be persisted in the save, or a
  replay runs under the wrong rules.
</Aside>

## Rendering: meshes, not sprites

<Badge text="WebGPU-only" variant="success" /> &nbsp; <Badge text="true 2:1 dimetric" variant="note" />

Terrain is drawn flat on a 32√ó16 dimetric grid with `x+y` painter's depth;
relief is a hillshade over a heightfield + fBm. Buildings, though, are **in-code
3D meshes** (2026-07-14) ‚Äî indexed triangle primitives fed to a software
rasterizer with back-face culling, a per-pixel z-buffer, and flat-shading onto
three adjacent Apollo palette steps. Deleting the old 2D ASCII-recipe path
removed **1,964 lines**. Night adds emissive window glow via the same day
factory, so lit and unlit frames can't drift.

## How it runs

Unlike Farm Valley, Citadel runs its sim in an **in-browser Web Worker**, posting
snapshots to the main thread over `postMessage` ‚Äî same transport-agnostic
`bootstrapSim()`, different pipe. (Multiplayer was deprecated, not deleted, in
2026-07-10 ‚Äî kept compiling as a revival target; solo is the product.)

<Aside type="note" title="Why a different palette">
  Citadel moved from EDG32 to **Apollo-46**: EDG32's gamut had no desaturated
  olive-grey midtones for muted medieval naturals. Because every colour already
  routed through named role constants with zero raw hex, the migration was an
  *import redirect* across ~200 sites, not a rewrite. See
  [Citadel decisions](/wiki/citadel-decisions/) and
  [Citadel rendering](/wiki/citadel-rendering/).
</Aside>
